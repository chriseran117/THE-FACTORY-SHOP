What We Do
Our Services
Cushion Works
We craft and replace all kinds of cushions — seat foam, back cushions, arm padding — using premium quality materials for lasting comfort and support.
Sofa Repairing
Broken frames, saggy springs, torn fabric, or damaged legs — our skilled technicians repair all sofa structural and upholstery issues to restore full functionality.
Sofa Cleaning
Deep professional cleaning that removes stains, dust, allergens, and odors — leaving your sofa looking fresh, bright, and hygienic. In-home service available.
Re-Upholstery
Give your sofa a whole new look with fresh fabric, leather, or velvet upholstery. Choose from a wide range of materials and colors to match your home décor.
Quality Guarantee
Every job we do comes with our commitment to quality. We use only the best materials and stand behind our craftsmanship for your complete satisfaction.
Home Pickup & Delivery
We offer convenient pickup and delivery for your sofa. No need to worry about transport — we handle everything right from your doorstep.
